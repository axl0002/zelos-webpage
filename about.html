About Zelos

The official calisthenics ranking app, built with and for the community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to unite the global calisthenics community through our platform. Where every athlete, from beginner to elite, can showcase their skills, climb the world's first global calisthenis leaderboard, and find inspiration through friendly competition and shared dedication.

Community

We exist to unite athletes around the world who share the same passion.

Competition

We push each other to new heights through fair and inspiring challenge.

Dedication

We show up every day, committed to progress, discipline, and the pursuit of mastery.

Our Story

Zelos started with a simple question: how do I as a calisthenics athlete compare to others around the world? Currently, calisthenics is a niche sport with few competitions held around the world each year meaning not everyone can compete.

We wanted to change that and allow everyone to have the opportunity to compete and showcase their hard earned skills on a global scale. From the first prototype to public release, every feature has been shaped by the community of real athletes.

  1. Aug 2025 - Research & Development

    MVP created and early Beta release.

  2. 24th Sep 2025 - iOS Launch

    300+ accounts created in the first 24 hours.

  3. 12th Oct 2025 - Android Launch

    Highly anticipated release building off the successful iOS launch.

  4. 22nd Oct 2025 - First 3000 Users

    Viral organic growth equating to over 1000 daily active users.
