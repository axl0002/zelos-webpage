Quick Start

  • Download Zelos on the App Store and create your profile.
  • Vote on athlete matchups to contribute to rankings.
  • Track your standing on global leaderboards.

Troubleshooting

  • Force-quit and reopen the app.
  • Check you’re on the latest version in the App Store.
  • Ensure network connectivity (Wi-Fi or mobile data).
  • If issues persist, email support@zelos.club with screenshots.
